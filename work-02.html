FLUVO는 말보다 표정으로 먼저 다가간다. 미세한 눈썹의 움직임은 기쁨, 망설임, 공감, 배려를 전달한다. 아이는 이러한 표정을 관찰하며 감정을 해석한다. 열선은 온도 변화를 통해 감정을 표현하여 아이가 촉감으로도 감정을 느끼게 한다. 벽걸이형 충전기와 프로젝터는 바닥과 벽을 놀이와 학습 공간으로 확장한다. 교육은 지시에서 참여와 경험으로 전환되며, 아이가 세상을 이해하는 방식을 넓혀간다.

English

Home is the first place where a child forms relationships, learns emotions, and practices society. As dual income families increase and parents hold different values, the balance of learning at home changes. Because of this shift, some children experience limited emotional interaction and fewer opportunities for social development. FLUVO was designed to reduce this gap. It is a robot for children aged five to ten, helping them understand emotions and build social awareness in life. Rather than compensating for insufficient education, FLUVO stays beside child. FLUVO approaches through expression before words. Subtle eyebrow movements convey joy, hesitation, empathy, and care. Children observe these expressions and interpret feelings. Heating elements communicate emotion through temperature changes, allowing children to sense feelings through touch. A wall mounted charger with projector expands floors and walls into spaces for play and learning. Education shifts from instruction to participation and experience, broadening how children understand world.
