Warigari Studio explores portable exhibition experiences that transcend the limitations of place, enabling engagement anytime and anywhere. Focusing on the ordinary moments of everyday life, we create intuitive and emotional experiences through an affordance-driven design approach, while exploring why such design is needed in today's society.
