FreeFlow is a cordless 2-in-1 handheld for car interiors. With one spin, it switches between vacuum and air-gun—no filter removal, no extra parts. Compact and balanced, it reaches tight spaces effortlessly. Blast, switch, vacuum, repeat—the freedom of air and the pull of suction in one tool.

English

Cleaning a car interior has its own rhythm: blast dust from seams, then vacuum it up—again and again in tight spots. FreeFlow is built for that flow. A cordless 2-in-1 handheld that switches modes with a single spin, rerouting airflow between vacuum and air-gun. No filter removal, no bin popping, no extra tool—just one seamless motion. Compact and balanced, it reaches consoles, seats, and door pockets with ease. FreeFlow keeps you in position and in focus, turning a stop-and-start routine into a smooth loop: switch, vacuum, blast, repeat. The freedom of air and the pull of suction—unified in your hand.
